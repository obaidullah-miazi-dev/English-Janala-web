Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by choosing a beginner, intermediate, or advanced level course. Simply browse through our lessons, practice exercises, and interactive activities, then start learning step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! Many of our lessons and practice activities are completely free. However, we also offer premium features and advanced courses for users who want extra learning support.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is optional for free content. But if you want to track your progress, save lessons, and unlock premium features, we recommend signing up for an account.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your vocabulary by practicing with our daily word lists, flashcards, interactive quizzes, and example sentences. Consistent practice will help you remember and use new words in real conversations.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we provide digital certificates for users who successfully complete our paid or premium courses. These certificates can help you showcase your English skills in academic and professional fields.
